Gender Equality Plan (GEP) Declaration

LAION e.V. is a non-profit association registered in Germany. In order to commitment to and foster a fair and inclusive research environment, we hereby adopt this Gender Equality Plan (GEP).

This declaration was formally adopted on 1 March 2026 by the Christoph Schuhmann, Jenia Jitsev and Robert Kaczmarczyk.

## Commitment areas

LAION e.V. commits to implementing concrete measures and targets across the following areas:

- work–life balance and an inclusive organisational culture
- gender balance in leadership and decision-making bodies
- gender equality in recruitment, retention, and career progression
- integration of the gender dimension into research and innovation content
- measures to prevent and address gender-based violence and sexual harassment

## Implementation

LAION e.V. will designate a responsible person or team to oversee GEP implementation, set measurable targets, and report on progress. This declaration will be reviewed and updated periodically to reflect evolving goals and outcomes.
